COLLABORATION POLICY

By registering on RACHANA, you are joining a special nationwide community of learners. The aspiration of RACHANA is to provide anyone in the country who has the motivation and ability to engage coursework from one of the top institutions and provides opportunity to attain the best educational experience that internet technology enables. You are part of the community which will help RACHANA achieve this goal.

RACHANA depends upon your motivation to learn the material and to do so with honesty. In order to participate in RACHANA, you must agree to the Honour Code below and any additional terms specific to a class. This Honour Code, and any additional terms, will be posted on each class website.


RACHANA HONOUR CODE PLEDGE

By registering on RACHANA, I agree that I will:

  • Use the content only for non-commercial purposes for my own learning. I will not submit the work of any other person.
  • Maintain only one user account and not let anyone else use my username and/or password.
  • Not engage in any activity that would dishonestly improve my results, or improve or hurt the results of others.
  • Not post answers to problems that are being used to assess student performance.
  • Unless otherwise indicated by the RACHANA, learners on RACHANA are encouraged to:
  • Collaborate with others on the lecture videos, exercises, homework and labs.
  • Discuss with others general concepts and materials in each course.
  • Present ideas and written work to fellow RACHANA learners or others for comment or criticism.
